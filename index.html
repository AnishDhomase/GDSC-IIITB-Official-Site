Google Developer Students Club

Indian Institute of Information Technology Bhopal

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome!

Get Started

What we do?

Google Developer Clubs can take many fms - from just a few people getting together to watch some videos, to large gatherings with demos, tech talks, hackathons and conferences. We work on various technical domains and try to make use of Google Technologies to empower the audience around us.

Connect

Meet students interested in developing technologies at IIIT Bhopal.All are welcome, including those with diverse backgrounds and different majors.

Learn

Learn about a range of technical topics and gain new skills through hands-on workshops, events, talks, and project-building activities - both online and in-person.

Grow

Apply new learnings to build great solutions for local problems. Advance your skills, career, and network. Give back to your community by helping others learn, too.

Events

Past Events


Web Dev Week

GDSC IIIT Bhopal organized an engaging WebDev Week for first-year students, spanning an entire week. The event delved into web and web 3.0 development, introducing participants to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for frontend stacks. The focus was on creating a static HTML page with design elements using CSS. Students gained insights into website functioning, crucial for a software career. Notably, hands-on projects guided by GDSC leads enhanced the learning experience. The first three days covered Web Development, while the final two explored Web 3.0 and decentralization. Day 4 included sessions on cryptocurrencies, Ethereum transactions, and Solidity programming language. The week successfully laid the foundation for students entering the development track.


Techsprint Quiz

Another flamboyant event of GDSC in NIIMACK'23 was TechSprint Quiz. As the name implies, TechSprint Quiz was a questionnaire on fascinating and talk-of-the town topics like machine learning, web development, cloud and general coding.
This event was held in two parts. The first part was an individual round and the second round was a team round.This event got the best of our participants as they were forced to unlock the full potential of their brain,while the clock was ticking down .After these two rounds ,the teams that emerged as top three were rewarded with GDSC goodies including T-shirts, Keychains,Coffee Mugs etc.
This event was not only fun in nature but also was a great tool in assisting learning as the audience were able to increase the levels of their knowledge.


Dev Insights

DevInsights was a technical talk session chaired by Mr. Aditya Shah, a member of GDG Cloud Bhopal and the organizer of WordPress Bhopal. This talk session was hosted by GDSC under the banner of NIIMACK'23.
The agenda of this talks session was to develop a thorough understanding of WordPress technology and the functionality of this technology. WordPress is the most popular platform among developers for building a website from scratch.
The event marked the attendance of more than 250 students and was an engaging and fruitful technical session.The students were then able to receive personal guidance from Mr. Shah himselves.


Hacktober Fest

Hacktoberfest 1.0 was an introductory session to divulge the enigmatic world of open-source contribution and version control.
This intro session was conducted by GDSC in a digital manner and topics like Git, GitHub and Open Source were covered in this session.In this session we touched ideas like git commands and the purpose they are used for.For a software developer, the importance of open source contribution and version control is extreme, and that is what we kept in our mind while bringing this session.
Further information about Hacktoberfest was shared to the students and they were told about how they can contribute projects in it as Hacktoberfest is a great platform to learn new things and hone your development skills while rewarding prizes to one with the most number of projects.


Cloud Study Jams

Students at IIIT Bhopal celebrated month long Cloud Study Jams from 1st of September to 1st of October. Cloud study jam are community run study groups for developers. It was facilitated by team GDSC.
The Cloud Study Jams had limited number of seats and saw a massive participation from the students. It was one of its kind as those who were successfully able to enroll for this event, got access to hands-on virtual labs which proved to be a great learning experience for all our students.
In Cloud Study Jam, there were two learning quest that students had to complete. First there was the Gen AI, arcade game and the second was Google Cloud Computing Platform (GCCP).It proved to be a really enriching experience for all those who participated in this study jam as we came to know about Cloud Clusters, Kubernetes engine and many other things.


VS Code Day

Visual Studio Code or VS Code is a code editor and integrated development environment for stimulating and debugging code snippets.
VS code happens to be a very integral and necessary software for students learning development and trying to become proficient in competitive coding. We at GDSC, took this responsibility on our shoulders to introduce VS Code to students of first year.
VS Code Day, was organised by team GDSC on 30th of September. This was an online event in which hands-on guidance was provided on how to setup VS Code properly and what all useful extensions need to be downloaded.
This was followed up by a QnA session in which the curiousity of our young coders, was put to a rest.
On this note, we wish that these young coders turn into software geeks after receiving the right guidance from GDSC.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the Google Developer Students Club?

    Google Developer Student Clubs are university-based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their communities.

  • What do we do?

    We at DSC IIIT Bhopal strive to make Developer content accessible to all, so that anyone can become an expert in Google Developer technologies. This program gives students access to resources and training that enables them to build new and exciting projects. We believe in the power of technology and community to empower people and build products for a better future.

  • Who can become a member?

    GDSC IIITB club is open to any IIIT Bhopal student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to enhance their skills furthermore .

  • How to connect with GDSC IIIT Bhopal?

    Join our local chapter GDSC IIIT Bhopal to know about upcoming events at GDSC IIIT Bhopal.

  • What are the advantages of becoming a member of DSC IIIT Bhopal?


    1. You will become a part of the worldwide student community.

    2. You get to learn how various Google Technologies work.

    3. You get a chance to participate in the DSC Solution Challenge 2022.

  • How frequently do events and workshops occur?

    GDSC IIIT Bhopal organises around 2-3 events every month. We recommend you to join our community to get updates on our workshops and events.

  • Who should I reach out to if I have any questions?

    If you have any questions, contact us by clicking the button below. We would be pleased to answer your questions.
